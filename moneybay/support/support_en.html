Frequently Asked Questions

To get started, simply download the MoneyBay app to your device. After opening the app, you can add your income and expenses to the respective categories. The app will automatically calculate and provide financial reports for you.

To add income or expenses, simply tap the "Add Transaction" button on the home screen. Then, select the transaction type (Income/Expense), enter the amount, and add a note if necessary. You can also select an expense category for more detailed tracking.

You can view your financial report in the "Reports" section of the app. Here, you will see graphs of income, expenses, and savings over different time periods (monthly, yearly). You can also view detailed expenses by category.

To export your expense data, go to the "Settings" section, select "Backup/Restore Data," then choose "Export to CSV." The data will be exported as a CSV file, which you can open on your computer or email for further analysis.

Yes, MoneyBay supports cloud data backup via iCloud. Simply enable the backup feature in the "Settings" section of the app to ensure your data is securely stored and can be restored when needed.

Yes, you can change the currency in the "Settings" section of the app. MoneyBay supports multiple currencies, and you can choose the one that suits your country and needs.

MoneyBay is committed to protecting your privacy. You can set a password or use fingerprint authentication to secure your data. Your data is stored safely and is not shared with any third parties without your consent.
